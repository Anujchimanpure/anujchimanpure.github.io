Research Paper: FRIDAY – Personal AI Companion

International Journal of Advanced Research in Science, Communication and Technology (IJARSCT)

In the rapidly evolving landscape of artificial intelligence, intelligent digital assistants have become a key area of research due to their potential to enhance user experience and simplify daily activities. This research paper presents F.R.I.D.A.Y. , a sophisticated personal AI companion designed to perform a wide range of tasks through natural and intuitive human interaction.

Unlike static software systems, F.R.I.D.A.Y. is designed as a dynamic and adaptive AI system that responds to user commands, learns usage patterns, and evolves with user needs. The assistant integrates voice recognition, speech synthesis, information retrieval, and task automation to provide seamless interaction between humans and machines.
